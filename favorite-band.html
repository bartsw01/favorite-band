Intro to Trank Harmony

Trank Harmony started in a Bulgarian blown out basement. The 5 women and 5 men met during a tornado storm. They huddled together in desperation in that fateful basement. While waiting for the storm to pass and emergency crew to rescue them, they found various items around them. The items include a big spoon, a sharp saw, a kettle, a trashbin, and a mop. They were bored so they decided to create some music for entertainment. Low and behold, the birth of their first hit, "Oh Crap, We're Gonna Die!" was born.
